About GFFI

The science behind financial crisis prediction

What is GFFI?

The Global Financial Fragility Index (GFFI) is an entropy-based indicator that measures the imbalance between market uncertainty and banking system resilience.

Formula: GFFI = (Entropy / Capital Ratio) × 1000

Key Findings

  • AUC Score: 0.921 (beats VIX 0.71)
  • Lead Time: 24 months before 2008 crisis
  • Validation: 14 countries, 6 major crises
  • Threshold: GFFI > 72.8 = Crisis alert
